Drupal - What is it?

  • Drupal is content management software.
  • Used to make many of the websites and applications you use every day.
  • Drupal project is open source software.
  • drupal.org

Drupal - Why do we use it?

  • It's free.
  • Your website is ready in 30 minutes.
  • You need no special knowledge.
  • If you maintain it well, this is safe.

Drupal - How it works?

  • Database oriented.
  • You create new articles with predefined forms.
  • You change style by downloading free templates.
  • You add extra modules to improve the behaviour of your site.

Drupal at UCL

  • Drupal is used at UCL.
  • UCL allows you to create easily your own website with Drupal.
